Peanutty Ice Cream Pie

Description

This is the recipe of the desert. A wonderfully easy to make and perfect to look at.

    Ingredients
  • Peanut
  • Vanilla flavoured ice cream
  • Sugar syrup
  • Chocolate chips
    Steps
  1. Take peanut and grind it into a fine paste
  2. Mix the peanut paste along with the ice cream
  3. Add sugar syrup
  4. Keep it in the freezer after giving it a perfect shape
  5. Dress it with chocolate chips and some sugar syrup
